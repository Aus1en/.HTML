Accountability.

How should we present a problem?

ALARMS
EVENTS

At PACS, we have experienced many customers needing constant TRACEABILITY of their process during runtime.

Constantly recording critical day to day operations of what is important for you to maintain, report, and create RELIABLE products has been a game changer for our customers.

This has allowed them to store an ACCURATE account of what has truly happened during critical operation.
Faults, speeds, setpoints, and whatever you deem important can be sent and stored to a database for a massive opportunity to increase all KPIs.

Alarming

Alarming is an important function when it comes to quality control and realtime adjustments to keep your process running at its max.

Creating understandable and precise alarming functions can be achieved by collaborating with the ones that run the process, and those who understand how it should work.

Events

Taking accurate records of what is actually happening from an events perspective would be crucial when tracing operational adjustments.

We can track every button click, screen adjustment, settings adjustment, database transactions, and other events that can be used to reference improvements or for training purposes.
